Gesture Controls

  • Draw: Extend your index finger to start drawing on the canvas.
  • Erase: Show three fingers out and move your hand over the drawing area.
  • Circle: Use two fingers and move in a circular motion to draw circles.
  • Rectangle: Use two fingers to create rectangular shapes by moving diagonally.
  • Tool Selection: Hover your hand over the toolbox to select tools and colors.
  • Adjust Thickness: Adjust the gap between the index finger and thumb.

Voice Commands

  • "Clear" - Erases the entire canvas.
  • "Screenshot" - Saves the current canvas as an image to your device.
  • "Increase Thickness" - Increases the pointer thickness for drawing.
  • "Decrease Thickness" - Decreases the pointer thickness for drawing.
